Insight → recommendation

LinkedIn punched above its weight. It reached ~40% fewer people than Instagram but drove the highest engagement rate (7.4%) and nearly as many sign-ups — the career-prep and privacy posts resonated with the student-professional audience.

Recommendation: shift one weekly Instagram slot to LinkedIn next week, and adapt the Friday blog (strong sign-up driver per click) into a LinkedIn carousel. Pause X promotion of feature posts — engagement is too low to justify the slot until creative is reworked.

Safe Money
LinkedIn · Tue Aug 26
🔒 Your data shouldn't be tuition fees for "free" apps As students, we hand over so much personal info without thinking twice — signing up for classes, downloading "study hacks," even just using campus Wi-Fi. 👀 But here's the catch: a lot of platforms trade that data like currency. That's why I care about using apps that put privacy first. With Safe Money, my savings stay mine — and so does my data. No selling, no sneaky tracking, just a tool that helps me stack up for goals (like career prep 👔 or travel ✈️) without worrying about what's happening behind the scenes. Because let's be real: peace of mind hits different when you know your money and your info are safe. 👉 Would you trust your savings with an app that doesn't value your privacy?
Safe Money
LinkedIn · Mon Aug 25
💸 Budgeting for Career Prep = Future You Winning We always budget for rent, food, coffee runs (because, priorities ☕). But how often do we budget for our career glow-up? 👀 Think about it: 📚 that course you've been eyeing · 👔 a fit that screams "hire me" · 📄 a resume/portfolio upgrade · 🌐 networking that actually moves the needle. These cost $$, and waiting till the last minute = stress. That's why I started a little "Career Prep Fund" on Safe Money. It's the easiest way to stash money aside without thinking about it — so when an opportunity pops up, I'm not scrambling, I'm ready. 🔑 Pro tip: investing in yourself isn't an expense, it's compounding returns. 👉 What would you save for in your career prep fund?

§ 00 — WHY
First Principles
THE PHYSICS OF LOSING
01
The Physics of Attention

Human attention collapses. On mobile, the abandonment threshold is 3 seconds. A WordPress site with heavy images takes 5–10 seconds on 4G. The user doesn't wait — they click Back. The client paid for the click. The website rejected the customer.

∴ We sell Zero Latency. Pre-computed HTML physically guarantees the fastest connection between the customer's brain and the Call button.
02
The Economics of Stability

A WordPress site is a dynamic machine with thousands of moving parts — database, PHP, plugins. It decays. It gets hacked. It requires constant maintenance money just to maintain the status quo. That's Technical Debt.

∴ We sell Immutability. A static site turns to stone on deploy. No SQL, no moving parts, no anxiety. An Asset, not a Liability.
03
The Geometry of Discovery

The internet for a local business is a 5km radius. A website floating in the void is useless. If the Google Business Profile is broken, the business is invisible — fighting for crumbs after the Map Pack takes 70% of clicks.

∴ We build the road (GBP), not just the house (site). We position the business in the path of highest probability.
04
The Psychology of Trust

In the absence of data, customers judge by proxies. A broken site = broken plumbing. The customer subconsciously infers competence from visual signals. They get the price-shoppers; premium clients go elsewhere.

∴ We sell Legitimacy. A one-man locksmith operation looks like a multinational on a phone screen. They charge more.
